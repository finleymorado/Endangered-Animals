Giant Panda

Giant pandas are native to China. They are known for their cute appearance, with black and white fur. They are herbivores, as well as solitary animals.

Diet

Giant Pandas eat almost only bamboo. Pandas can eat 25 to 40 pounds of bamboo daily, with it taking up 99% of their diet. The other 1% of their diet is mostly other plants. Pandas tend to eat every part of the bamboo plants, incuding the leaves, stems, and shoots.


Habitat

Giant Pandas only live in China! They live in the mountain regions of south-central China. Pandas cna be found in the Sichuan, Gansu, and Shanxi provinces.


Vulnerable

In September of this year, giant pandas have downgraded from "Endangered" to "Vulnerable" on the official list of threatened species. This has been made possible by the WWF working with the government to help establish new reserves and other wildlife conservation efforts.
