Animals all over the world are endangered, putting them at risk of extinction. Factors like habitat loss, climate change, pollution, and poaching pose a threat to wildlife, and the balance they contribute to their ecosystem.

Raising awareness about endangered species is crucial for their protection. Our goal is to educate about the threats these animals face, as well as the consequences ecosystems may face. By promoting conservation efforts, a difference can be made by protecting these species.
